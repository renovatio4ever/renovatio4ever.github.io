Welcome to Janken Pon!

Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors game. Otherwise known as "Janken Pon". Did you know that Janken Pon was invented thousands of years ago in China? Indeed, the game became popular over the millenia until it reached Japan around the 1700's and eventually the rest of Western Civilization. There are serveral variations of the game including one that involves a FOURTH selection (not included in this game) that results in an automatic loss! This is a simple HTML/JS variation of the game. First player to hit twenty wins the game. Enjoy, and good luck :) - Pete
