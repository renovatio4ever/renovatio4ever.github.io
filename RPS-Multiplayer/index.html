Welcome to Janken Pon II

Welcome to the Janken Pon II. The game was re-vamped from the original RPS Assignment, and now features firebase to support multiplayer, observer, and chat capabilities.

For first time visitors, did you know that Janken Pon (now called Rock, Paper, Scisscors) and was invented thousands of years ago in China? Indeed, the game became popular over the millenia until it reached Japan around the 1700's and eventually the rest of Western Civilization. There are serveral variations of the game including one that involves a FOURTH selection (not included in this game) that results in an automatic loss! Enjoy, and good luck
